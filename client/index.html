What is WEBOT?

WEBOT is an AI-powered chatbot that can converse with humans in natural language. It uses the latest in natural language processing and machine learning to understand and respond to user queries.

The possibilities are beyond your imagination
  • Education: WEBOT can help students with their homework and answer their questions in real-time. It can also be used as a virtual tutor to help students learn new subjects.
  • Healthcare: WEBOT can be used to help patients schedule appointments with doctors and answer their medical queries. It can also help doctors with medical diagnosis and suggest treatment options based on symptoms.
  • E-commerce: WEBOT can be used to help customers find products they are looking for and suggest alternatives. It can also provide customer service and help resolve issues with orders.
  • Finance: WEBOT can be used to help customers with their banking queries and provide financial advice. It can also be used to detect fraudulent activities and prevent financial crimes.
